KSB Purchase Requisition System — User Manuals

How to use: Find your role below, open the page, then print to PDF from your browser.

  1. Click your role.
  2. Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac).
  3. Choose "Save as PDF" as the destination.
  4. Paper size: A4. Margins: Default. Background graphics: ON.
  5. Save.

Role-specific manuals

1. Initiator

Any staff member creating Purchase Requisitions, EFTs, Petty Cash, or Expense Claims.

2. HOD (Head of Department)

First-stage approver for your department's requests.

3. Finance / Finance Manager

Second-stage approver; manages budgets, FX rates, and GRN approvals.

4. MD (Managing Director)

Final approver on high-value Purchase Requisitions.

5. Procurement

Vendor management, quote collection, and adjudication.

6. Stores User

Warehouse staff creating GRNs, Issue Slips, and Picking Slips.

7. Administrator

User accounts, vendors, clients, budgets, and system settings.
